Each of these folders contains parameter list as required by RangeShifter. 

RangeShifter v1.0.5 was used, with software and user manual available in the
supplementary material of the article below:
	Bocedi, G.... (2014). RangeShifter: A platform for modelling spatial eco-evolutionary 
	dynamics and species’ responses to environmental changes. Methods in Ecology and Evolution, 
	5(4), 388–396. https://doi.org/10.1111/2041-210X.12162
